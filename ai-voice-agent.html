Common questions about AI voice agents, answered directly.

Q

What is an AI voice agent?

An AI voice agent is software that handles phone calls using natural language understanding and text-to-speech. It listens, understands intent, and responds conversationally, replacing IVR phone trees with actual dialogue. It can book appointments, answer questions, qualify leads, and route calls to humans when needed.

Q

Can an AI voice agent really handle phone calls?

Yes. Modern AI voice agents manage thousands of calls daily across industries. They handle multi-turn conversations, understand context, and complete real tasks. When they encounter something beyond their scope, they transfer the call to a human agent with full conversation context.

Q

How natural does an AI voice agent sound?

Remarkably natural. We use advanced text-to-speech models that replicate human cadence, intonation, and rhythm. Most callers cannot tell the difference. The voice is customizable for tone, accent, and speaking style to match your brand.

Q

What happens if the AI can't answer a question?

The AI voice agent has built-in escalation protocols. It acknowledges the caller's request, gathers relevant details, and transfers to the appropriate human with a full conversation summary. The caller never repeats themselves.

Q

How much does an AI voice agent cost?

Pricing depends on call volume, conversation complexity, and integrations. Most businesses see 40-60% cost savings versus human agents. There is a one-time setup fee plus monthly usage based on minutes. Book a discovery call for a detailed quote.

Q

Can it book appointments in my calendar?

Yes. The AI voice agent integrates with Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, and most practice management systems. It checks real-time availability, books the slot, and sends confirmation messages. No double-bookings.

Q

What languages does the AI voice agent support?

English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and more. The agent can detect the caller's language and switch automatically, ideal for businesses serving multilingual communities.

Q

How long does setup take?

Standard deployments go live in 2-4 weeks. Simple appointment-booking agents can launch in as little as 10 days. Complex multi-department setups with deep CRM integrations take 4-6 weeks. You get weekly progress updates throughout.
